How it works

Three steps, no signup, no credit card. Telegram is the only thing you need.

1

Open the bot

Tap "Open in Telegram" above. The bot starts you on a 7-day free trial with full access to every feature.

2

Speak or type

Send a voice message in your target language, or just type. The bot understands both, in any of 6 languages.

3

Hear the answer

You get a text reply, an audio explanation, and a separate clip with the correctly-pronounced target-language phrases.

What makes this different

Most language tutors mispronounce target-language words when they're embedded inside an English explanation — the TTS engine stretches one language's phonemes over the other. The result is unusable audio for the part you actually need to hear.

This bot solves it. Every reply comes as TWO voice messages: one with the explanation in your native language, and a second one with ONLY the target-language phrases, generated in a native voice with the correct phonemes. You can play it back as many times as you need.

It's the only Telegram bot we know of that does this. Hear it once and you'll see why the difference matters.

What you can do

Six modes. Pick whichever matches what you want to practice today.

Structured lessons

/lesson — step-by-step curriculum that builds vocabulary and grammar progressively.

Free conversation

/conversation — chat at your level. The bot adapts to what you've already learned.

Role-play scenarios

/scenario — order at a café, check into a hotel, ask for directions. Realistic situations.

Spaced review

/review — spaced repetition of your weakest areas, focused on what you actually need to drill.

High-frequency vocab

/vocab — learn the most common words at your level first. The 80/20 of vocabulary.

Video + quiz

/video + /quiz — comprehensible-input video at your level, with a quiz to check.
